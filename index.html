How to play

At the start of each round, you will be given a random number that you must match by clicking on the gems below. When you click on a gem, its value will be added to your score. If you exceed the random number, you lose the round! If you match the number, you win!!

But, don't forget - the value of each gem is hidden and changes at the start of a round.
